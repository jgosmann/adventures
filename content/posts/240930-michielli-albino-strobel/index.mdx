The first via ferrata that we are doing is the Via Ferrata Michielli Albino
Strobel (B/C) in the west face of the Punta Fiames. After the bus dropped us off
in Fiames, we start ascending at quarter to ten. At some point we miss a sharp
turn, but notice soon as the trail disappears.

The start of the via ferrata can be described as alpine. Before a proper steel
cable starts, one already has to do a little bit climbing. The via ferrata
itself also has almost no artificial foot steps or steel rungs. This is quite
nice and prompts one to climb a lot on the actual rock.

a bit scree left before we get on a proper trail, but from there it gets easier.

about 970m and went down even more today.
