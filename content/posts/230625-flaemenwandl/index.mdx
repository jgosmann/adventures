At this year's summary retreat, I am again one of the organizers of the rock
climbing activity. Like <Link to="/posts/220703-flaemenwandl/">last year</Link>,
we go to the Flämenwandl. We are a quite large group this time with a total
of 18 people. For the beginners we install top ropes on some easy routes. The
more advanced people can also lead climb on some moderate climbs. Even the
organizers have time for some enjoyable climbs.
