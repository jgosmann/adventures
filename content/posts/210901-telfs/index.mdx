Finally, the low pressure influences left for good and we have beatiful, warm,
sunny weather. Today we do the Leite via ferrata near Nassereith (difficulty
C/D). After a short bus ride it is a couple of minutes to walk to the base of
the via ferrata.

<Loc latlong={[47.31689, 10.84839]} zoom={12} />

The via ferrata goes mostly across slabs and there are a few spot where you have
to rely on the friction of your shoes created from the opposition of forces by
pulling the steel rope and pressing with your feet.

Nassereith before catching our bus back. During the lunch we see a cyclist doing
some longer tour with a lot luggage.
